I'm [Nico](/), an engineer and designer based in London, UK. I'm the founder of Atelier, an AI studio building [liquid software](/software-is-liquid) for high-performance teams. 

Previously, I led the personalisation team at Healf, a £100M+ company building the future of wellbeing intelligence. I also spent time building early Gen AI tools at Sapient AI Labs, and personalised learning interfaces at Educai.

I write about software, design, and (occasionally) personal growth. My posts tend to be short and infrequent.

## Page design

This site follows Steph Ango's [file over app](https://stephango.com/file-over-app) philosophy. It is built with a custom Python static site generator and uses the [Flexoki](https://stephango.com/flexoki) color scheme — an inky palette inspired by analog printing. The design prioritises readability and simplicity.

<p class="pro-tips">Pro tips: press <strong>T</strong> to toggle the theme between light and dark, <strong>S</strong> to open search, or <strong>H</strong> to jump home.</p>
